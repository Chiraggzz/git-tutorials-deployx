# Blueprint: Desktop Publishing
### Book for 1st Semester Students

---

## Book Title
**Desktop Publishing: Concepts and Practice**
*A Beginner's Guide for 1st Semester Students*

---

## Preface
This book introduces 1st semester students to the fundamental concepts and practical skills of desktop publishing (DTP). It covers the history, tools, design principles, and hands-on workflows needed to create professional print and digital documents.
